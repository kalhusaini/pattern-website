About This Property

A luxurious serviced apartment in WKN 25, located in the distinguished Al Ghadeer district. This property offers a complete living experience in a prime Riyadh location — thoughtfully designed for comfort, convenience, and elevated daily living. The apartment features three bedrooms including a dedicated office and reading room, a spacious living area that opens onto a private terrace, three well-appointed bathrooms, and a fully equipped laundry room. The kitchen comes complete with everything you need. Rent includes electricity, water, high-speed internet, building services, and a dedicated parking spot.

About This Property

Banan Retreat is a private escape just outside the city — purpose-built for gatherings, celebrations, and quiet evenings under the stars. The property features a grand majlis that comfortably seats up to 22 guests, surrounded by beautifully landscaped gardens with palm trees and ambient lighting. A separate building houses a private bedroom, a full kitchen, and a small dining area — perfect for overnight stays. With parking for up to 10 cars, this retreat is designed to host with ease and leave lasting impressions.

استراحة بنان ملاذ خاص خارج المدينة — مصمّمة للجمعات والاحتفالات والسهرات الهادية تحت النجوم. تتميز بمجلس كبير يتسع لـ ٢٢ شخص بكل راحة، محاط بحدائق منسّقة بأشجار النخيل وإضاءة مميزة. مبنى مستقل يحتوي على غرفة نوم خاصة، مطبخ كامل، وصالة طعام صغيرة — مثالي لمن يبغى يبات. مواقف تتسع لـ ١٠ سيارات، والمكان كله مصمّم عشان تستضيف براحة وتترك انطباع ما ينتسى.
